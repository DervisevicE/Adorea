How it works


Double-clicking the button, you connect your jewelry with the application on your phone via bluetooth.

Your location is automatically sent to desired contacts via the application using the Internet, or via SMS if the Internet is unavailable.

Your desired contacts receive a notification in the form of a message with a recognizable ring and a loud vibration. They can see where you are at the moment and track your movement if you specify that in the settings.

App Features

Selection of 5 trusted contacts

After purchasing our product, you choose 5 people who will be your safe contacts in emergency situations.

Easy to Customize

By adding your profile on application, you can edit trusted contacts, enable shared locations, add pictures of you and your contacts, and select various premium benefits that interest you.

Previously recorded fake calls

If you feel insecure while walking alone or your loved ones don't answer your phone, you can use this feature to feel safe.

Video and sound recording

After double-clicking on the product, audio and video recording will automatically start on your mobile phone using the camera, so you will have proof that the attack took place and you will be able to use it later to identify the person.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I need to have my cell phone with me?

    It depends on the version of the product you have. If you have a version of the jewelry that connects to your phone and doesn't have a SIM card built into it then you have to have mobile phone. If you have jewelry that has a sim card built into it, you don't have to.

  • Waht happens if I accidentally press the button?

    Nothing will happen because the location is activated in two clicks.

  • Can I change my contacts at any time?

    Yes. You can change your constacts in setting section.

  • Is there a version for men?

    We currently only have a version for women, but we are working on releasing a collection for men as well.

  • Does Adoréa always use my location?

    No, your location will only be used when you click on jewelry.

  • Is there a version for children?

    We currently only have a version for women, but we are working on releasing a collection for children as well.
